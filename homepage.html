Lost and Clueless!

Introduction

Hello! Welcome to a small game created by the following developers; Andrew Seneca, Hunter Diebler, Chad the man,
Kyle Dormus, and Jacob Winter. The game that we have created for you to play and enjoy is a simplistic
adventure game that takes you through a couple of questions to ask in order to achieve the end.
But be careful, your flashlight is alwauys running out of battery, and when your flashlight goes out the darkness
will allow monsters and creatures to kill you in the dark. But fear not there are batteries around the map that
you will be able to find to keep yourself alive.

The setting of this game takes place within the lost valleys of Nahani. This valley is covered with forests,
however there are a few trails that lead you through this haunted, foggy, dark valley. But the trail happens,
to be the only safe area to walk into. For years people have ventured into the woods with very few ever returning.
They call the Nahani valley, the "Valley of Questions," it is believed through the very few survivors that the valley
makes you ask questions in order to survive and leave. Many people have brought flashlights inside the forest. Meaning
if you ever run low on your battery life you can try and scavenge for more batteries in order to stay alive.
